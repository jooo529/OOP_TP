<Mission 2>
You have to make some fruitboxes! 
You can learn 'value and variable's relation' in this mission.

If you have fruits,
(=if you have value)
You can put fruits in Box.
(=you can save value in variable)


You must have each fruits like this :

Apple :      4 or More! makes one AppleBox!
Grape :      2 or More!   makes one GrapeBox!
Orange :     4 or More!   makes one OrangeBox!
Strawberry : 8 or More!   makes one StrawberryBox!
